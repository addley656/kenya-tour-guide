Welcome to TembeaPwani

Explore Kenya's breathtaking coastline with a professional local guide. Discover beaches, history, culture and unforgettable adventures.

Popular Coastal Destinations

Diani Beach

Kenya's most famous beach with white sand, turquoise waters and exciting water sports.

Watamu

Explore marine parks, coral reefs and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling.

Malindi

Experience rich Swahili culture, beautiful beaches and historical attractions.

Nyali

Enjoy modern coastal living, shopping, entertainment and beautiful beach views.

Lamu

Discover ancient Swahili architecture, donkey transport and unique island culture.

Fort Jesus

Visit one of Kenya's most famous historical landmarks and learn about coastal history.
